About Me

As an Executive Sales Engineer with Square D by Schneider Electric since 2011, I specialize in delivering advanced power distribution, automation, and energy management solutions for contractors in Tulsa, Oklahoma, and Northwest Arkansas. Holding a B.S. in Engineering from Tennessee Tech University, I leverage technical expertise to provide tailored solutions that enhance efficiency and sustainability for a diverse range of projects.

At Square D, I focus on a comprehensive portfolio, including power distribution equipment, automation and power monitoring solutions, software, residential load centers, and commercial/industrial power systems such as low and medium voltage switchgear, panelboards, transformers, and drives. My work supports critical industries, including healthcare, water/wastewater, industrial facilities, schools, hospitals, hotels, retail buildings, and commercial properties, ensuring reliable and innovative energy solutions.

I am passionate about building strong partnerships with contractors to drive operational excellence and sustainability. Connect with me on LinkedIn to explore how Square D’s solutions can support your projects.
